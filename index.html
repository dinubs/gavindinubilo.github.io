Hackathon Vignette

Chat Room

The only place she can feel a spark or a connection with someone is on the Internet. She’s tired of being played by guys who think she’s cute. She’s just looking for a break in life, but all she does is spend her time in questionable chat rooms online. This girl’s name is Maggie; she doesn’t know me, and I don’t know her. She thinks I’m someone else, some famous chocolate rapper. She says I’m sweet like an M&M. I am not sweet-I am just a Sour Patch Kid. I can tell her what I think about the world, and she’ll believe me because I’m famous. I’m finally running the show and I can change the direction of the conversation at the drop of a hat. How did I get here? It started from a link in the source code, to parsing out the 800 unique emails, to using creative writing to impersonate the persona of someone famous.

Signed,

Whoever I am.
